Some singleton $\{x\}\subseteq X$ is an open set.

*Note*: A space that fails to satisfy {P139}, 
i.e., a space without any isolated points, is called "dense-in-itself" or "crowded". 
See {{wikipedia:Dense-in-itself}} and {{mathse:3823020}}.

Compare with {P107}.
