---
uid: P000241
name: Locally a closed half-line $\mathbb R_{\ge 0}$
aliases:
  - Locally a $1$-Euclidean half-space
---

Each point has a (not necessarily open) neighborhood homeomorphic to {S210},
or to the closed half-line $\mathbb R_{\ge 0}$.

This is the special case of {P236} with $n=1$.

Note that {P155} spaces satisfy this property
since every $x\in\mathbb R$ has $[x-1,x+1)$ as neighborhood.

Equivalently, each point $p\in X$ satisfies one of the following:
- $p$ has an open neighborhood homeomorphic to {S25};
- $p$ has an open neighborhood homeomorphic to {S210}
with $p$ mapped to the element $0\in[0,1)$.

----
#### Meta-properties

- This property is preserved by arbitrary disjoint unions.
