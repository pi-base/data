There is a covering space $(E,p)$ of $X$, such that $E$ is {P200}. Defined on page 484 in {{zb:0951.54001}}.

By Corollary 82.2 in {{zb:0951.54001}} this is equivalent to $X$ being {P224}, {P37} and {P42}.
