A space with a countable dense subset.

Defined on page 7 of {{zb:0386.54001}}.

----
#### Meta-properties

- $X$ satisfies this property iff its Kolmogorov quotient $\text{Kol}(X)$ does.
- This property is preserved in any coarser topology.
