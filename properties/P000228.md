---
uid: P000228
name: Weakly first countable
---

For all $x \in X$, there is a sequence of subsets $(V_n(x))_{n \in \mathbb{N}}$ with $x \in V_n$ for all $n\in \mathbb{N}$, such that $O \subseteq X$ is open iff for all $x \in X$ there is a $n \in \mathbb{N}$ such that $V_n(x)\subseteq X$.

----
#### Meta-properties

- This property is hereditary.
- $X$ satisfies this property iff its Kolmogorov quotient $\text{Kol}(X)$ does.
- This property is preserved by arbitrary disjoint unions.
