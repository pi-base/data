---
uid: P000245
name: Has a finite topology
aliases:
    - Has finitely many open sets
---

The cardinality of the topology on $(X, \tau)$ is finite, i.e. $|\tau| < \infty$. 

Equivalently, the Kolmogorov quotient of the space is finite.

----
#### Meta-properties

- This property is hereditary.
- This property is preserved in any coarser topology.
- The Kolmogorov quotient $\text{Kol}(X)$ is {P78} iff $X$ is {P245}.
- This property is preserved by finite products.
