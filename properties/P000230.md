$X$ admits a basis of open sets which are {P200}.

Equivalently, for each $x \in X$, every neighborhood of $x$ contains a simply connected open neighborhood of $x$.

Defined on page 298 of {{zb:1209.57001}}, page 495 of {{zb:0951.54001}} and as property $P_1$ of {{mo:487326}}. Has also been called "strongly locally simply connected", for example in {{zb:1030.57035}}.
