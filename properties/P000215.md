Every subspace is {P162}. 

Equivalently, $X$ is {P6} and $X\setminus \{x\}$ is realcompact for each $x\in X$. (theorem 8.17 of {{zb:1380.46022}})

----
#### Meta-properties

- This property is hereditary.
