Every closed {P52} subset $A\subseteq X$ can be covered by a family of pairwise disjoint open subsets of $X$, each meeting $A$ in only one point.

Defined for example on pages 382 and 640 of {{zb:0546.00022}}.

----
#### Meta-properties

- $X$ satisfies this property iff its Kolmogorov quotient $\text{Kol}(X)$ does.
- This property is preserved by arbitrary disjoint unions.
