For any two disjoint zero-sets are contained in disjoint clopen sets.

We say $Z\subseteq X$ is a *zero-set* when $Z = f^{-1}(0)$ for some continuous $f:X\to\mathbb{R}$.

Also see theorem 6.2.4 in {{zb:0684.54001}}.
