name: Subcompact
---

*from discussion at STDC, will add refs later*

There exists a basis $\mathcal B$ of nonempty sets
such that for every regular filter base
$\mathcal F\subseteq\mathcal B$, $\bigcap\mathcal F\not=\emptyset$.

A regular filter base $\mathcal F$ has the property that for each
$A,B\in\mathcal F$, there exists $C\in\mathcal F$ such that
$\operatorname{cl}(C)\subseteq A\cap B$.
