A space for which every subspace is {P26}.

Defined on page 182 of {{doi:10.1016/B978-0-444-50355-8.X5000-4}}.

----
#### Meta-properties

- $X$ satisfies this property iff its Kolmogorov quotient $\text{Kol}(X)$ does.
- This property is hereditary.
