---
uid: P000227
name: Has closed discrete subset of size $\mathfrak c$
---

There exists a closed discrete subset of cardinality $\mathfrak c$.

Equivalently, Has a closed discrete subset of size $\geq \mathfrak c$

Equivalently, every set $S \subseteq X$ of cardinality $\mathfrak c$ has a limit point in $X$.

Compare with {P107}, {P21} and {P198}.
