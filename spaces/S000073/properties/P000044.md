$A = \{(0,0)\} \cup ((0,1) \times (0,1))$, which is connected, as $(0,1) \times (0,1)$ is (being Euclidean) and $(0,0)$ is in its closure. Similarly $\{(1,0)\} \cup ((0,1) \times (0,1))$ is connected. And $X = A \cup B$.
