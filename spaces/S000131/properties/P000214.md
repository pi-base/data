For $n \in \omega$, let $S_n:=\{n\}\times \omega$. Then $S_n$ converges to $\infty$.
But every $S \subseteq \bigcup_{n \in \omega}S_n$ with $|S \cap S_n|\geq 1$ for infinitely many $n$  has points on infinitely many columns on $\omega \times \omega$ and thus cannot converge to $\infty$.
