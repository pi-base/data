The disjoint union $X = \bigsqcup_{i\in I}\mathbb{Q}$, $|I| = \mathfrak{c}$, of continuum many copies of {S27}.

See {{mathse:3947741}}.

This space is homeomorphic to the product $D\times\mathbb Q$
with $D$ = {S3} (see {{mathse:2726886}}).
