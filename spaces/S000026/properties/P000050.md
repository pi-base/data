For each $\sigma \in 2^{<\omega}$, let $[\sigma] = \{ x \in 2^\omega\ |\ x \text{ extends } \sigma \text{ as a sequence} \}$. Then $\{ [\sigma]\ |\ \sigma \in 2^{<\omega}\}$ is a clopen basis.
