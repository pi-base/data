The space $X = \mathbb{Q} \cup \{\infty\}$ is the Alexandroff one point compactification of {S27}.
The open sets in $X$ are the open subsets of $\mathbb Q$ together with the sets $X\setminus C$ with $C$ compact in $\mathbb Q$.
