If $(a_i),(b_i) \in U \subset \mathbb{R}^\omega$ for a basic open set $U$ then $f: t \mapsto \big(a_i + t(b_i - a_i)\big)$ is an arc in $U$ from $(a_i)$ to $(b_i)$.
