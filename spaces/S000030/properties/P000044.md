Let $\pi:\mathbb{R}^\omega \rightarrow \mathbb{R}$ be projection onto the first coordinate. If $A = \pi^{-1}((-\infty,0))$ and $B = \pi^{-1}([0,\infty))$ then $A$ and $B$ are connected and $A \cup B = \mathbb{R}^\omega$.
