Fix a point $(p,q)\in [0,1]\times [0,1]$.  Consider all open intervals $((a,b),(c,d))$ containing $(p,q)$ with all of $a,b,c,d$ rational, or $a=p$ or $c=p$.  This is a countable basis at $(p,q)$.
