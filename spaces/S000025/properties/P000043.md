Let $B_\epsilon(x) = \{y\ |\ d(x,y) < \epsilon\}$. $\{B_\epsilon(x)\}$ is a local basis at $x$ and since each $B_\epsilon(x)$ is convex, it is arc connected via the straight line arc between any two points.
