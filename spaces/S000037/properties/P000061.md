The Tychonoff reflection of $X$ is {S36},
with the reflection map $\varphi:X\to\mathbb [0,\omega_1]$
sending $\omega'_1$ to $\omega_1$ and every other element to itself.

Since {S36|P61}
and the reflection map $\varphi$ is open,
the result follows from Proposition 2 in {{mathse:5108293}}.
