It is enough to show that each open set in $X$ is {P30}.
The whole space is {P30} since {S209|P16}.
And a proper open subset of $X$ is a disjoint union of open sets,
each homeomorphic to either {S25} or {S83}.
Both {S25|P30} and {S83|P30}.
Since the {P30} property is perserved by taking disjoint unions, the result follows.
