Let $Y\subseteq X$.

If $Y \cap \omega_1 \times \omega_1 \neq \emptyset$, this follows from the definition.

Otherwise there is an $A \subseteq \omega_1$ such that $Y = A \times \{\omega_1\}$. Let $p = \inf A$,
then $\{\langle p, \omega_1 \rangle\} = Y \cap (-1,p] \times (0,\omega_1]$ is open in $Y$.
