For $a \in X$ consider the open neighborhood $U_a := [0,a+1)\subseteq X$. By the definition of $X$, $U_a$ is countable ordinal space and thus metrizable, see [explore](https://topology.pi-base.org/spaces?q=countable+%2B+ordinal+%2B+%7EMetrizable).
