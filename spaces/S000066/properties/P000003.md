For any points $x , y\in\mathbb R^2\setminus\{0\}$ there are neighborhoods $U$ and $V$ separating $x$ and $y$, since {S176|P3}.
For $x = 0$ and $y = 0^*$ take $U=V_1(0)$ and $V=V_1(0^*)$.
