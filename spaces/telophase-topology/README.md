name: Telophase Topology
---
Let $(X, \tau)$ be the topological space formed by adding to the ordinary closed unit interval $[0,1]$ another right end point, say $1^{\ast}$, with the sets $(a,1) \cup \{1^{*}\}$ as a local basis.

See also:

* Steen, L. A.; Seebach, J. A. (1970), [Counterexamples in Topology](http://books.google.com/books/about/Counterexamples_in_Topology.html?id=DkEuGkOtSrUC), Dover, p 92.
