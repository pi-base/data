Let $C \subset X$ be closed. If $p \not\in C$, $C$ is open and so $G_\delta$ trivially. If $p \in C$, let $A_n = (\{n\} \times \omega) \setminus C$. Then $B_n = X \setminus A_n$ is open and $C = \bigcap_n B_n$ is $G_\delta$.
