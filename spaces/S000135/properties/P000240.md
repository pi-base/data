It is a $1$-dimensional {P240} with $X_0 = \left\{ x \in X \mid \left\|x\right\|_2 \in \mathbb N \right\} \subset X_1 = X$,
where the sets $1$-cells are segments connecting $(n \cos \theta, n \sin \theta)$ and $((n + 1) \cos \theta, (n + 1) \sin \theta)$ for each $n \in \mathbb N$ and $0 \leq \theta < 2 \pi$.
