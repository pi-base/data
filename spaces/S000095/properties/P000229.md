$C_1 \subset X$ inherits its topology as the unit circle $S^1 \subset \mathbb{R}^2$. $C_2 \subset X$ inherits the discrete topology. Thus the path components of $X$ are $C_1$ and $\{x\}$ for each point $x \in C_2$.

The result follows since {S170|P229} and {S162|P229}.
