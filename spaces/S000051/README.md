The integers $\mathbb Z$ with basis $\{\{2i-1,2i,2i+1\}\mid i\in\mathbb{Z}\}\cup\{\{2i+1\}\mid i\in\mathbb{Z}\}$.

It is the unique irreducible {P193}, i.e. a cut point space where no proper subset is a cut point space.
