$X$ is a subspace of the product $\prod_{n\in\omega}(\omega_{n+1}+1)$ with the box topology consisting of all $f\in \prod_{n\in \omega}(\omega_{n+1}+1)$ such that $\omega< \text{cf}(f(n))$ for all $n\in\omega$ (see [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cofinality#Cofinality_of_ordinals_and_other_well-ordered_sets) for definition of cofinality).

Equivalently, $X$ is the Hewitt realcompactification of {S138}, 
as shown in {{doi:10.4064/fm-73-2-179-186}} (see remark 8.8 of {{doi:10.1007/978-1-4615-7819-2}} for definition of Hewitt realcompactification).
