Witnessed by the standard proof that the space is not {P13}:
The countable closed set $\{(q,-q):q\in\mathbb Q\}$ and closed set
$\{(p,-p):p\in\mathbb R\setminus\mathbb Q\}$ cannot be separated by
open sets.
