Let $ x\in{X} $ then $ |\{x\}|=1 $ and thus $ X\backslash{\{x\}} $ have finite complement meaning that $ X\backslash{\{x\}} $ is open and thus $ {x} $ is closed meaning that $X$ is $ T_1 $.
