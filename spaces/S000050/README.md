  - Non-Hausdorff cone over the rationals

Let $X = \mathbb{Q} \cup \{\infty\}$, where $\mathbb{Q}$ has the usual topology on {S27} and is an open subset
of $X$, and where the only neighborhood of $\infty$ is $X$. See {P202}.
