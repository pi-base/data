Each $f:\omega\to\mathbb R$ is the countable intersection of
$\bigcap_{m<\omega}\prod_{n<\omega}(f(n)-\frac{1}{m+1},f(n)+\frac{1}{m+1})$.

See {{mathse:1644041}}.
