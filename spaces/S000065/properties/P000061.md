The Tychonoff reflection of $X$ is {S158},
with the reflection map $\varphi:X\to\mathbb [0,1]$ sending $1^{\ast}$ to $1$
and every other element to itself.

Since {S158|P61}
and the reflection map $\varphi$ is open,
the result follows from Proposition 2 in {{mathse:5108293}}.
