Let $x\in L_1$ and $U$ a neighborhood of $x$.  Then we see that there is an index $N$ such that $U\cap R_n\ne \emptyset$ for all $n\ge N$.  Then $A=U\cap R_N$ and $B=U\setminus R_N$ form a separation of $U$.
