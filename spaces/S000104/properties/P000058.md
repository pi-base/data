$| [ 0, \Omega ) \times I^I | = | [0,\Omega ) | \cdot | I^I | = \aleph_1 \cdot |I|^{|I|} = \aleph_1 \cdot ( 2^{\aleph_0} )^{2^{\aleph_0}} = \aleph_1 \cdot 2^{\aleph_0 \cdot 2^{\aleph_0}} = \aleph_1 \cdot 2^{2^{\aleph_0}} = 2^{2^{\aleph_0}} = 2^\mathfrak{c} > \mathfrak{c}$.
