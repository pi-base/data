Given $x\in X$, there is a largest point $y\in X\setminus\{x\}$.
Every nonempty open set of the subspace $X\setminus\{x\}$ contains $y$,
and therefore $X\setminus\{x\}$ is {P39} and thus {P36}
[(Explore)](https://topology.pi-base.org/spaces?q=Hyperconnected+%2B+%7EConnected).
