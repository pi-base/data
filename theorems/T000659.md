First observe that a {P208} {P3} space is {P52}, since every subset is compact, hence closed.

Now, if $X$ is {P208} and {P134}, its Kolmogorov quotient is {P208} and {P3}, hence {P52}.  This is equivalent to $X$ being {P185}.
