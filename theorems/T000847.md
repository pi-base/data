For each $x\in X$, every neighborhood of $x$ contains an open neighborhood homeomorphic to
some Euclidean space $\mathbb R^n$ or some closed half-space $\mathbb R^n_+$.
And both $\mathbb R^n$ and $\mathbb R^n_+$ are {P199}
as they can be deformation retracted to a point using a straight-line homotopy.
