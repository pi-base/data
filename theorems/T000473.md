Follows from Theorem 3.2 of {{doi:10.1016/0016-660X(76)90024-6}}.
Given a decreasing local base $\{B_n:n<\omega\}$ at a point $x$, the winning strategy
at $x$ for Player 1 is to play $B_n$ during round $n$.
