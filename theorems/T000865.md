Let $A$ be a nonempty closed irreducible subset of $X$.
By {P51} there is a point $x$ isolated in $A$.
Since $A$ is {P39}, the singleton $\{x\}$, which is open in $A$, is dense in $A$, i.e., $x$ is a generic point of $A$.
This shows that $X$ is {P192}, and thus also {P73}
