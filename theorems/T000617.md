If $p \in X$ is a cut point, then $X \setminus \{p\} = U \cup V$ with $U, V$ disjoint, nonempty, and open in $X \setminus \{p\}$. For $X$ to be hyperconnected, $U \cup \{p\}$ and $V \cup \{p\}$ must be open in $X$, so $\{p\}$ must also be open in $X$.
