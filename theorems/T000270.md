Let $\mathcal{B}=\{U_n\}_{n \in \omega}$ be a countable basis for $X$. Then for any $x \in X$, $\{U \in \mathcal{B}\mid x \in U\}$ is a countable local basis at $x$.

See 16.1 of {{mr:2048350}}.
