If $X$ is Lindelöf and a $G_\delta$ space, every open set in $X$ is an $F_\sigma$ and hence is also Lindelöf.
This uses the facts that closed sets in a Lindelöf space are Lindelöf, and a countable union of Lindelöf subspaces is Lindelöf.

See Theorem 16.6 in {{zb:1052.54001}}.
