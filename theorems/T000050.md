Let $D$ be a countable dense subset of $X$. Then the map $\Phi : X \rightarrow 2^{P(D)}$ by $\Phi(x)(A)=1$ if and only if $A=D \cap U_x$ for some neighborhood $U_x$ of $x$ is injective if $X$ is Hausdorff.
