Let $p$ be the possible non-isolated point from {P203}. Every point
$x\neq p$ has $\{x\}$ as a local base.

Let $(V_n(p))_{n\in\mathbb N}$ be the weak-base sequence at $p$ from
{P228}. Each $V_n(p)$ is open: if $y=p$, then $V_n(p)\subseteq V_n(p)$.
If $y\neq p$ and $y\in V_n(p)$, then $\{y\}$ is open, so by {P228} some
$V_k(y)\subseteq\{y\}\subseteq V_n(p)$. The openness criterion in {P228}
therefore applies to $V_n(p)$.

If $U$ is an open neighborhood of $p$, then {P228} gives an $n$ with
$V_n(p)\subseteq U$. Thus the sets $V_n(p)$ form a countable open local
base at $p$. Hence $X$ is {P28}.
