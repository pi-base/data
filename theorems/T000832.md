Consider the collection of closed set $\mathcal{C}:=\{\operatorname{cl}(C)\mid \text{$C\subseteq X$ is countable}\}$ and let $\operatorname{cl}(D)$ be a maximal element thereof. Suppose $\operatorname{cl}(D) \neq X$, then choose some $x \in X \setminus \operatorname{cl}(D)$ and we get $\operatorname{cl}(D)\subsetneq \operatorname{cl}(D\cup \{x\}) \in \mathcal{C}$, contradiction.
