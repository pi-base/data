Then the game is played on $\mathbb R^n$ and Player 2 can win because $\mathbb R^n$ is {P206} [(Explore)](https://topology.pi-base.org/spaces?q=Completely+metrizable+%2B+%7EEmpty+%2B+%7EStrongly+Choquet).
