\(T_3\) is often defined as regular + \(T_1\), but on page 12 of
{{zb:0386.54001}} the authors note that regular + \(T_0\)
implies \(T_2\), and therefore \(T_3\) (using their usual reversed
language surrounding \(T_x\) axioms).
