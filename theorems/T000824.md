Assume $X$ were infinite. Pick distinct $x_1,x_2,\dots\in X$. Then $\{x_1\}\subsetneq \{x_1,x_2\}\subsetneq\{x_1, x_2, x_3\} \subsetneq \dots$ is an infinite ascending chain of closed subsets, contradiction to {P226}.
