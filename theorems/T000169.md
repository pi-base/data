By definition on page 33 of {{doi:10.1007/978-1-4612-6290-9}},
given two discrete points \(x,y\), the subspace \(\{x,y\}\) has
an isolated point, say, \(x\). Thus \(\{x\}\) witnesses \(T_0\).
