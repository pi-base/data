In a $T_1$ space points are closed.  By Urysohn's Lemma, for any point $a$ and closed set $B$ disjoint from $a$, there is a continuous function $f:X \to [0,1]$ sending $a$ to 0 and $B$ to 1.

See page 17 of {{zb:0386.54001}}.
