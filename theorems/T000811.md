Because $X$ is infinite, we can write $X = X_1 \cup X_2$ where $|X| = |X_1| = |X_2|$.
Then $X_1, X_2$ are homeomorphic to $X$ which is {P36} ([explore](https://topology.pi-base.org/spaces?q=222+%2B+not+78+%2B+not+36)).
