If $X$ is totally separated and $a,b \in X$, let $a \in U$ and $b \in V$ with $U,V$ open and disjoint and $X=U \cup V$. Define $f:X \rightarrow [0,1]$ by $f(U)=\{0\}$ and $f(V)=\{1\}$. Then $f$ is a continuous function with $f(a)=0$ and $f(b)=1$.
