Let $x \in X$ and $A$ the neighborhood kernel of $x$.

For every $y \not\in A$ we find an open neighborhood $U_y$ of $x$ not containing $y$.
Since $X$ is countable, so is $S := \{U_y\mid y \not\in A\}$. By {P147}, this implies $\bigcap S = A$ is open, which implies the assertion.
