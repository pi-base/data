Let $A$ be a countable subset of $X$.
Since $X$ is {P168},
$A$ is closed in $X$ and hence {P19}.
Furthermore, the subspace topology on $A$ is discrete.
So $A$ is necessarily finite.
This proves that $X$ itself is finite.
