Let $p \in X$ be the only non-isolated point. The subspace $X \setminus \{p\}$ is {P52} and {P208},
hence {P78} [(Explore)](https://topology.pi-base.org/spaces?q=Discrete+%2B+Noetherian+%2B+%7EFinite).
Therefore $X$ is also {P78}.
