Let $p$ be the unique non-isolated point.
Then $p \in \overline{X \setminus \{p\}}$.
Let $D \subseteq X \setminus \{p\}$ be countable with $p \in \overline{D}$. Then $\{\{x\}: x \in D\}$ forms a countable local $\pi$-base for $p$.
