If $X$ is {P144}, {P134} and {P30}, its Kolmogorov quotient is {P82}, {P3} and {P30}.
By the "Smirnov metrization theorem" (Theorem 42.1 in {{zb:0951.54001}}), a space with the last three properties is {P53}.
Hence $X$ is {P121}.
