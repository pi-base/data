Any set in a {P57} {P2} space is a $G_\delta$ set,
since its complement is an $F_\sigma$ set as a countable union of singletons.

If $X$ satisfies the hypotheses, $X\times X$ is also {P57} and {P2}.
Thus the diagonal in $X\times X$ is a $G_\delta$ set.
