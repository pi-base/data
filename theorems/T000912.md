For any $x \in X$, define $U_x = \bigcap \{ U \in \tau : x \in U \}$ to be the minimal neighborhood of $x$. Since the topology is finite, the intersection is finite, so $U_x$ is open and hence in $\tau$. 
The map $x \mapsto U_x$ is injective by $T_0$, so $|X| \leq |\{U_x : x \in X\}| \leq |\tau| < \infty$, so $X$ is finite.
